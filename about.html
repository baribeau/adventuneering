About

Adventure Engineering: a blog dedicated to sharing inspiring examples of sustainable innovation and resilient problem-solving, from anywhere in the world.

Hey, nice to meet you! I'm Julia Baribeau, a graduate from Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo whose passions lie in sustainability, community, and adaptability. I hold a particular love for water.

I've had the awesome opportunity to do lots of backpacking in the last year, in South/Southeast Asia, Europe, and Canada. I learned about so many exciting projects during my travels, from massive city-scale water treatment plants to ancient sustainable agriculture strategies. This blog is an attempt to share my learnings with as many people as possible, to inspire action, innovation, and hope for a sustainable future.
